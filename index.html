ABOUT ADGITM

Dr. Akhilesh Das Gupta Institute of Technology & Management , formerly known as Northern India Engineering College (NIEC), New Delhi was established by BBDES, LUCKNOW in the year 2003. ADGITM has an aesthetically designed campus spread over approximately 8.08 acres of lush green environment at Shastri Park. The Campus comprises of five interconnected blocks covering a built up area of approx. 23,636.67 square meters. ADGITM offers following Under Graduate and Post Graduate level full time Professional programs approved by AICTE and BCI in affiliation with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), New Delhi.

Courses Offered:
  • Bachelor of Technology
  • Master of Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Business Administration
  • Integrated BA-LLB (Hons.)
  • Integrated BBA-LLB (Hons.)
